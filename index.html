Privacy Policy for Polytown Extension

Last updated: May 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Polytown Extension (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is published by Jad Mintun. This Privacy Policy applies to the Polytown Extension (beginning with version 0.0.38) available from the Chrome Web Store.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

3. Permissions

  • storage: Used only to save your extension settings locally in your browser.
  • tabs: Used only to detect which tab is active so that the extension button can update its state.

4. Third‑Party Services

This extension does not use any analytics, advertising, or other third‑party services that collect data.

5. Data Retention & Security

No data is collected, transmitted, or stored on any server. All settings remain on your local device.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have any questions about this policy or your rights, see the contact section below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a revised “Last updated” date.

8. Contact Us
